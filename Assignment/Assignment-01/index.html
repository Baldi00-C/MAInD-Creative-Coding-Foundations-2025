ADHD Out of the Chat! is the ultimate digital brain dump for when your actual brain is, well, in the chat with like 17 other things.

It helps you:

  • Organize & Schedule your life so you can actually enjoy it.
  • Stop the doom scrolling (at least about your to-do list).

Basically, we're taking the chaos out of the group chat and putting it into one clean, chill space.

You don't need a 5-step tutorial, promise.

Here's the drill:

  • Add Text: type your task in the input box.
  • Flag It: mark urgent tasks.
  • Delete/Modify: remove or edit tasks easily.

Your brain is for big ideas; this app is for remembering the small stuff.
